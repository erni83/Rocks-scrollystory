Heat, Work, & Worker Protection in Central America

Extreme heat is a growing problem in agriculture in Central America, driving migration and hurting outdoor workers. La Isla Network storytellers went to Guatemala and El Salvador to investigate the problem and learn about the solution that LIN is advancing.

Photographs by Ed Kashi / VII / Redux. Film by Tom Laffay.

CHAPTER I

This chapter is part of a broader visual story on labor, heat exposure, and worker protection in El Salvador's sugarcane sector. Photographs by Ed Kashi & Tom Laffay were developed in collaboration with La Isla Network and supported by the Rockefeller Foundation to document and advance practical solutions that protect workers and strengthen resilience under rising heat conditions.
